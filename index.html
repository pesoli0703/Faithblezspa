Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
Yes, to ensure we provide you with uninterrupted, premium service without wait times, all sessions are strictly by appointment. You can book easily via WhatsApp.
How long is each session?
Our sessions typically range from 45 minutes to 90 minutes, depending on the treatment you select. We also offer extended sessions upon request.
Can couples book together?
Absolutely. We offer specialized couples massage packages in a private, romantic suite designed for two. Perfect for anniversaries, date nights, or just reconnecting.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept bank transfers, POS payments at the facility, and all major credit/debit cards for your convenience.
Where exactly are you located?
We are located in a secure, serene environment in Ikoyi, Lagos. Exact directions and address will be provided upon appointment confirmation for privacy and security.
Can I reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can reschedule. We kindly ask for at least 12 hours' notice so we can adjust our therapists' schedules accordingly and accommodate other guests.
