Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?
Yes, to ensure we provide you with uninterrupted, premium service without wait times, all sessions are strictly by appointment. You can book easily via WhatsApp.
How long is each session?
Our sessions typically range from 45 minutes to 90 minutes, depending on the treatment you select.
Can couples book together?
Absolutely. We offer specialized couples massage packages in a private, relaxing suite designed for two.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept bank transfers, POS payments at the facility, and major credit/debit cards.
Where are you located?
We are located in a secure, serene environment in Ikoyi, Lagos. Exact directions will be provided upon appointment confirmation.
Can I reschedule?
Yes, you can reschedule. We kindly ask for at least 12 hours' notice so we can adjust our therapists' schedules accordingly.
